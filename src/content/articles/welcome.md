This is the first post on The Duke Post — a tech publication built on a simple idea: original writing where it matters, and an honest, fast-moving feed of what everyone else is reporting, clearly labeled as such.

## How content works here

There are two kinds of posts on this site:

1. **Articles** — written here, by us. Opinion, analysis, explainers.
2. **The Wire** — a curated feed of headlines from other publishers, refreshed regularly. We link out; we don't republish.
